Techopedia-XI

Techopedia is the annual technical festival organised under IEEE Student Chapter of SIES GST. It is a Nationwide festival that is held to encourage participation from student members as well as student advancement via entertaining and interesting activities and events. Techopedia XI will be held offline this year revolving around our theme "ILLUSION - A GLITCH IN THE MATRIX".

Frequently Asked Question

Techopedia is the annual technical fest of IEEE SIES GST. This year we are celebrating its Eleventh edition.

To commemorate Techopedia's eleven-year triumph, nothing is more difficult than the concept of glitches and illusions. We wish to travel through a variety of illusions and mistakes for Techopedia 11.0 in order to improve our vision of reality and problem-solving skills. Our objective is to explore the world of errors and working our best to derive the solutions, separating fiction from reality and implementing the facts.

By simply participating you increase the likelihood of winning exciting cash prizes, coupons, goodies and national-level certificates.

The Registration Fee is only 80rs for team of two members.

You can keep an eye out for updates on our social medias’ linked below and join our WhatsApp group after registration to keep a track of the happenings

On-day registrations are unavailable! So register before the closing date for incredible fun through Techopedia XI.
